Assets were never meant to stop at export

Modern 3D assets move through design reviews, visualization workflows, commerce experiences, XR platforms, manufacturing systems, and increasingly AI-driven tools. Every transition creates opportunities for materials to drift, variants to split, dependencies to break, and work to be rebuilt. XYZO helps assets stay organized, reusable, and trustworthy as they move.

What stays connected

XYZO keeps the important parts of a 3D asset connected so teams can keep working instead of starting over.

Structure

Keep geometry, hierarchy, naming, variants, and presentation states organized for continued iteration.

Dependencies

Keep textures, references, and supporting files gathered into stable, portable asset structures.

Materials

Keep material assignments and supporting evidence inspectable through review, refinement, and handoff.

Variants

Keep options, colorways, concepts, and iterations organized without duplicating entire assets for every change.

Review outputs

Create lightweight outputs for fast review while keeping the source asset structure intact.

Validation

Surface what passed, what changed, and what needs attention before an asset moves downstream.

Why this matters now

3D asset creation is accelerating. AI tools, new visualization platforms, commerce needs, and immersive experiences are increasing the number of assets teams need to manage. More assets mean more handoffs, more versions, more variants, and more ways for context to get lost. XYZO helps teams keep the work usable as it moves.
