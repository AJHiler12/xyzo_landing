What XYZO packages

XYZO turns source 3D files into portable asset packages that preserve structure, materials, dependencies, variants, and handoff context.

What an XYZO Asset Package preserves

Source context

Key file origins and packaging context stay attached so teams can review what was handed off and where it came from.

Localized dependencies

Linked textures, references, and supporting files are gathered into a portable package that is easier to move across tools and teams.

Material evidence

Material assignments and supporting evidence are preserved to help teams inspect visual fidelity and resolve handoff uncertainty faster.

Variant structure

Named options and structural variation remain intact so downstream reviewers can understand what changes across asset states.

Viewer-ready payloads

Packages can include reviewable payloads that make inspection and handoff easier without requiring a heavy internal pipeline stack.

Validation and handoff reports

Each package can carry reports that document packaging outcomes and give collaborators a clearer basis for approval, revision, or reuse.

Why AI-generated 3D needs validation

AI-generated 3D can accelerate concept and content creation, but teams still need a reliable way to confirm that materials, linked assets, and package structure hold up in handoff. XYZO helps turn those files into portable packages with clearer trust signals before they move downstream.
