What XYZO packages

XYZO turns source 3D files into portable modular asset packages that preserve structure, materials, dependencies, variants, and validation context for continued review, refinement, and handoff.

What a Modular Asset Package preserves

Editable structure

Keep asset organization intact so teams can continue refining geometry, materials, variants, and presentation states.

Localized dependencies

Gather textures, references, and supporting files into a portable package with stable paths.

Material evidence

Preserve material assignments and supporting evidence so visual decisions remain inspectable through handoff and review.

Variant logic

Keep options and iterations organized without duplicating entire files for every change.

Review-ready payloads

Generate lightweight viewer outputs while keeping the authoritative asset structure intact.

Validation and handoff reports

Document what changed, what passed validation, and what needs attention before the asset moves forward.

Why AI-generated 3D needs validation

AI-generated 3D can accelerate concept and content creation, but generated files still need structure, material checks, dependency control, and validation before they can become reliable parts of an iterative creative workflow.
